A Fashionista's Solar Noon Calculator

For reasons that entirely have to do with fashion, I take many pictures of myself. However, I do not have control over my lighting considering that I shoot outside. So I try to shoot photos at solar noon, the point in the day when the sun is at its highest. This reduces shadows.

This application tells me when solar noon occurs for the current day.

Demonstrates my skill in Python, Django, and Javascript (particularly AngularJS).
